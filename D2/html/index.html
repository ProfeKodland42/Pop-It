What is Pop It?

Pop It literally translates as "pop it". It’s a children’s game similar to popping bubble wrap. But unlike bubble wrap, Pop It can be pressed endlessly with characteristic sounds and sensations: dozens of silicone bubbles just pop through to the other side.

Game Rules

Players take turns pressing the bubbles. You must always finish the row you started. You can press a maximum of 3 bubbles at once (they must be adjacent). The goal is to make your opponent go last. Whoever makes the last move loses!
